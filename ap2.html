Laparoscopic bariatric & metabolic surgery

Programs Info

Program Name Laparoscopic bariatric & metabolic surgery
Hospital Name China Medical University Hospital
Medical Specialties Others
Hospital Network Central Taiwan
Introduction Body science and Metabolic disorders International (B. M. I.) Medical Center is comprised of two sections: the Metabolic Medicine, and the Aesthetics and Wellness, to encompass the elements of weight loss and diabetes surgery, body contouring, and anti-aging medicine.
Achievements

Metabolic Medicine offers a variety of surgical procedures to address weight loss and diabetes, including the single-port laparoscopic gastric bypass, sleeve gastrectomy, and gastric-banded plication. Other complex procedures are also available to cater to the patients’ individual specific needs such as sleeve gastrectomy with loop duodenojejunal bypass or proximal jejunal bypass.

Dr. Chih-Kun Huang is very involved in research and education. He had trained more than 60 international fellows.

B.M.I Medical Center is a high-volume center, with an estimated 400+ cases of bariatric surgery performed every year, as well as, we are a referral center for many cases with complications. As an established international healthcare center, we integrate clinical, research, and educational functions. Currently, we are conducting a combined research with the psychology and family medicine departments.
Duration
  • Course 1: 1~3 months, 5 person
  • Course 2: 3~12 months, 5 person
  • Course 3: 3~12 months, 5 person
Program fee Course: NTD 5000/month
Accommodation Other, Lodging and meals not included
Applicant Qualification
  • Valid Physician/Medical License
  • More than one year of working experience
Learning Targets
  1. Bariatric & metabolic surgery
  2. Bariatric patient care
Learning Skills
  1. Orthognathic Surgery
  2. Cleft Lip and Cleft Palate
  3. Microtia surgery
